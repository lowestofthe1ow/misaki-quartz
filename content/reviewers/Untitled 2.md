1.
$$
\cos\left(10x+\frac{\pi}{6}\right)+\cos\left(10x-\frac{\pi}{4}\right) = 1.5867\cos\left(10x-\frac{\pi}{24}\right)

$$
7\cos\left(20x+\frac{\pi}{3}\right)+4\cos\left(20x-\frac{\pi}{6}\right) = \sqrt{65}\cos\left(20x+0.5281\right)
